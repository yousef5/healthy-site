Healthy Pharmaceutical & Medical Industries

We are a fully integrated pharmaceutical entity, covering all of Egypt through a robust distribution network of over 2,500 pharmacies, 1,000 employees, and 200 vehicles. Through our pharmacy chain "Moneer Pharmacies" based in Mansoura, we deliver a trusted, seamless pharmaceutical experience directly to our customers. We proudly own "Healthy Cure", our scientific office dedicated to developing high-quality medications such as Omepure and Germytin.
