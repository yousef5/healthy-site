Who We Are

Healthy Pharmaceutical & Medical Industries is a pioneering pharmaceutical company dedicated to improving healthcare across Egypt. With decades of experience in pharmaceutical manufacturing and distribution, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry.

What We Do

We manufacture and distribute high-quality pharmaceutical products, dietary supplements, and medical supplies. Our comprehensive portfolio includes HealthyCure brand products such as Omepure, Germitin, AlfaMore, and many more trusted medications.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality pharmaceutical products and services to all communities across Egypt, ensuring the well-being of our customers through innovation, integrity, and excellence.

Our Vision

To be the leading pharmaceutical company in Egypt and the Middle East, providing innovative healthcare solutions that improve the quality of life for our customers.
